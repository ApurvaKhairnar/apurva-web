About

Hi, I’m Apurva, a Master’s in Management student at the Prague University of Economics and Business with a background in International Journalism and Communication. Over the past few years, I’ve built a diverse portfolio as a content creator and strategist, working with leading agencies and brands like Neutrogena, Amazon, Nivea, and Star Network.

My passion lies in crafting content that’s not only creative but also performance-driven. I specialize in ideating and writing for short-form video across social media platforms – from snappy captions and scripts to full storyboards and campaign messaging. I love combining insight-driven strategy with a creative touch to build content that’s engaging, relevant, and thumb-stopping.

With experience spanning diverse sectors, I bring a well-rounded perspective and adaptability to every project. I’m always eager to explore new challenges, grow creatively, and contribute meaningful, results-driven work in the world of content and communication.
